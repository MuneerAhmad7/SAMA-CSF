## 1. Purpose
Define the organization's commitment, principles, and mandatory requirements for protecting information assets, in compliance with SAMA Cyber Security Framework and NCA Essential Cybersecurity Controls.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all employees, contractors, vendors, and third parties who access, process, store, or transmit organizational data or systems, across all business units and locations.

## 3. Policy Statement
The organization is committed to protecting the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of its information assets through a risk-based cybersecurity program aligned to SAMA CSF, NCA ECC, and applicable Saudi law (PDPL).

## 4. Governance
- A Chief Information Security Officer (CISO) is appointed, independent of IT, reporting to the [CEO / Board Risk Committee].
- A Cyber Security Committee meets at least quarterly and reports to the Board.
- Cybersecurity strategy is reviewed and re-approved annually.

## 5. Supporting Policies (referenced, maintained separately)
- Access Control Policy
- Data Classification & Handling Policy
- Incident Response Policy
- Third-Party & Cloud Security Policy
- Acceptable Use Policy
- Business Continuity & Disaster Recovery Policy
- Cryptography & Key Management Policy

## 6. Roles & Responsibilities
| Role | Responsibility |
|---|---|
| Board / Risk Committee | Approve strategy & policy; receive quarterly reporting |
| CISO | Own program, report to Board, manage exceptions |
| Cyber Security Committee | Cross-functional oversight, risk decisions |
| All employees | Comply with policy, complete awareness training, report incidents |
| IT Operations | Implement technical controls |
| Internal Audit | Independent assurance |

## 7. Compliance & Enforcement
Non-compliance may result in disciplinary action up to termination, and where applicable, legal action. Exceptions require documented, time-bound approval from the CISO.

## 8. Review Cycle
This policy is reviewed at least annually or upon significant regulatory, organizational, or threat-landscape change.

## 9. Acknowledgment
All personnel must acknowledge this policy at onboarding and annually thereafter. Acknowledgment is tracked in the Governance Tracker (`Policies` module, `acknowledged_by` log).
