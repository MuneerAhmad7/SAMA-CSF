# Cybersecurity Governance RACI Matrix
**Maps to:** SAMA CSF 1.4, NCA ECC 1‑4‑2

R = Responsible, A = Accountable, C = Consulted, I = Informed

| Activity | Board / Committee | CISO | IT Ops | Legal/DPO | Internal Audit | All Staff |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Approve cybersecurity strategy | A | R | I | C | I | — |
| Approve cybersecurity policy | A | R | C | C | I | I |
| Appoint CISO | A | I | — | C | — | — |
| Conduct annual risk assessment | I | A | C | C | I | — |
| Approve risk treatment / acceptance | A | R | C | C | I | — |
| Cybersecurity awareness training rollout | I | A | R | C | I | R (complete) |
| Incident response execution | I | A | R | C | I | R (report) |
| Board cybersecurity reporting (quarterly) | A | R | I | I | I | — |
| Internal cybersecurity audit | A (receives) | C | C | I | R | — |
| External audit / SAMA-NCA examination coordination | A | R | C | C | C | — |
| Third-party / vendor security assessment | I | A | R | C | I | — |
| Patch management SLA compliance | I | A | R | — | I | — |
| Access review (quarterly privileged) | I | A | R | C | I | — |
| Policy exception approval | I | A | C | C | I | — |

### Notes
- Only **one A per row** — accountability must not be shared.
- This matrix should be version-controlled alongside the policy and reviewed at each policy review cycle.
- Import into the tracker's `RACI` module for live tracking and change history.
